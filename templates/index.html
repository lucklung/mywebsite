Empowering the Next Generation to Lead with Ethical AI

YouthGood.ai is a youth-led global initiative helping students not just use AI, but shaping it responsibly through education, real-world discussions, and leadership opportunities.

AI is shaping our future, but most students are only taught how to use it, not question it. YouthGood.ai exists to change that. We empower students to think critically about AI, understand its societal impact, and become leaders in building a more fair and responsible future.

Meet the Founder

Lukas Leung
Founder, YouthGood.ai

Lukas is a high school student and builder who believes the next generation won’t just use AI, they will shape it.

He started YouthGood.ai after realizing that while AI tools were becoming widely accessible, very few students were being taught how to think critically about their impact. Questions around fairness, bias, and responsibility were often overlooked.

Through initiatives like the Y.E.A.H. program and the AI Mirror tool, Lukas is working to make ethical AI education accessible to students everywhere, not as a technical skill, but as a leadership responsibility.

His goal is simple: to empower young people to become not just users of AI, but thoughtful creators of a more responsible future.
