YEAH Student Ambassador Program

YEAH (Youth for Ethical AI & Humanity) is a student-driven movement launched under YouthGood.ai to ensure that artificial intelligence is used for learning, not shortcuts; for support, not surveillance; and for empowerment, not exploitation.

Through free educational GPTs, public outreach, and collaboration with educators, YEAH empowers student leaders across California to lead peer discussions and workshops focused on promoting ethical and responsible AI use.
