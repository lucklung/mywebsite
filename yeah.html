Y.E.A.H. (Youth Empowerment Through AI for Humanity)

The Y.E.A.H. Program is an initiative under YouthGood.ai that empowers youth leaders to learn and promote the responsible and ethical use of Artificial Intelligence in education and society. It's a completely free program to join in! At the end of the 4 week session, students who participate will be able to earn a certificate of the Y.E.A.H. program! We hope this program allows students to walk away with more experience in ethical AI and are able to see the real-world implications that artificial intelligence effects.

Y.E.A.H. members gain leadership experience, build teamwork skills, and engage in meaningful conversations about how AI can shape a more equitable and creative future.

The Fall Y.E.A.H. Program Cohort is officially closed! Keep an eye out for the Spring Y.E.A.H. Program Cohort
